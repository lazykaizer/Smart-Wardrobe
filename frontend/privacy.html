Privacy Policy

Smart Wardrobe - AI-Powered Clothing Management

Last Updated: October 5, 2025

Important: Smart Wardrobe stores all your data locally in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Smart Wardrobe ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our AI-powered clothing management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored in hashed form)
  • Account creation date

2.2 Wardrobe Data

Information about your clothing items, including:

  • Item names and descriptions
  • Clothing types and categories
  • Colors and seasonal information
  • Tags and custom labels
  • Images you upload (if applicable)

2.3 Usage Data

We may collect information about how you use the Service:

  • Feature usage patterns
  • Outfit generation preferences
  • Style choices and recommendations you view

3. How We Store Your Information

Local Storage: All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage technology. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We cannot access your wardrobe information
  • You have complete control over your data
  • Clearing your browser data will delete all stored information

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Generate AI-powered outfit recommendations
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Communicate with you about the Service

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 No Third-Party Sharing

Since your data is stored locally on your device, we do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for their commercial purposes.

5.2 Academic Use

As this is a final year academic project, aggregated and anonymized data may be used for educational purposes, presentations, or research. No personally identifiable information will be included.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are not stored in plain text
  • Local storage provides browser-level security
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • Regular security reviews and updates

Note: While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and browser.

7. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your stored data at any time
  • Modify: Edit or update your information
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your wardrobe data
  • Control: Manage your browser's localStorage settings

8. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device until you:

  • Delete your account
  • Clear your browser's localStorage
  • Uninstall or clear your browser data

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Smart Wardrobe uses localStorage for data storage. We do not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies. Your browsing activity within the app is not tracked or monitored.

10. Third-Party Services

The Service may use third-party libraries and frameworks for functionality:

  • Font Awesome for icons
  • Google Fonts for typography
  • Spline for 3D visualizations

These services may have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

11. Children's Privacy

Smart Wardrobe is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

12. AI and Machine Learning

Our AI-powered features use your wardrobe data locally to generate recommendations. The AI processing happens within your browser, and no data is sent to external AI services or servers for processing.

13. Data Backup

Important: Since data is stored locally, it is not automatically backed up. We recommend you:

  • Regularly export your wardrobe data
  • Keep track of your clothing items elsewhere if needed
  • Understand that clearing browser data will delete all information

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Providing notice through the Service

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

15. Academic Project Notice

Smart Wardrobe is developed as a final year academic project for educational purposes. The Service is provided as-is, and we make no guarantees regarding long-term data availability or support beyond the academic term.

16. International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data protection laws in your jurisdiction apply. We do not transfer data across borders as no server-side storage is used.

17. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through:

  • The contact information provided on our website
  • The project team's official communication channels

18. Your Consent

By using Smart Wardrobe, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Service.

Summary: Your privacy matters to us. Smart Wardrobe keeps all your data on your device, giving you complete control. We don't collect, share, or sell your personal information. You can delete your data anytime by clearing your browser storage.
